Black Octagon Technologies

Research & Training on Internet and Digital Safety in Myanmar

About Us

Black Octagon Technologies is a research and training group working on internet and digital safety in Myanmar. We study internet access, online scams, censorship, and VPNs.

We help people stay safe online and understand how to access information freely. We also run workshops and trainings for students, community groups, and NGOs.

What We Do

  • Monitor internet shutdowns and blocked websites
  • Investigate online scams and digital fraud
  • Teach safe internet use and secure tools such as VPNs
  • Run easy-to-follow workshops and trainings on digital safety

Our Mission

To make the internet safer and easier to use for everyone in Myanmar.

Our Vision

A Myanmar where people can access information freely and protect themselves online.
